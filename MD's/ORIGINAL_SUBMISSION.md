## Idea
RailConnect: Intelligent Multi-Train Journey Recommendation System

India's vast railway network connects thousands of destinations, yet passengers traveling between locations without direct train services often struggle to identify reliable multi-train routes. They must manually compare train schedules, estimate delays, assess connection feasibility, and plan long waiting periods, making journey planning complex and uncertain.

RailConnect is an intelligent journey planning system that automatically discovers the best multi-train travel combinations between any source and destination. Using historical train movement data, it predicts connection reliability and recommends routes optimized for travel time, waiting duration, and likelihood of successful transfer.

Beyond route planning, RailConnect acts as a smart travel assistant. For long layovers caused by train schedules or delays, it recommends suitable facilities and stay options at interchange stations based on waiting time, time of day, passenger needs, and available amenities such as waiting halls, retiring rooms, lounges, food courts, and nearby accommodation.

By enabling passengers to confidently utilize indirect rail connections, RailConnect improves access to underserved destinations, reduces travel uncertainty, enhances utilization of existing railway infrastructure, and strengthens citizen access to public transportation without requiring additional railway resources.

The solution supports Digital India's vision of citizen-centric digital services by making railway travel more accessible, reliable, and convenient for millions of passengers.
